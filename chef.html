Our Master Chef

Chef Ange Smith has studied culinary arts at the University of Chicago. She has served as a chef at the Red Stone Restaurant since graduating with honors. Chef Ange was trained under former master chef, Chef Doug Smith, for seven years before attaining the title of master chef herself. Know that with Chef Ange in the kitchen, you are in for a treat.
