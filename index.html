Greetings!

It is our pleasure to welcome you to the Red Stone Restaurant, your home away from home. Our food is designed for adventurers like you and will give you the pep you need to dive head first into the Redstone Forest. Whether you are venturing out to experience all that the Forest has to offer, spending the night at Camp Flora, or you are just in the neighborhood, stopping by our establishment is a must. We are eager to enrich your adventures with our delicious "Red Fish Medley".

All of our guests are family and will receive all the comforts of home. Our meals are dedicated to the adventurous lifestyles of our customers and designed to allow you a moment's rest from your busy life. Let us show you a new meaning of Rest and Relaxation with our famous "Homely Hospitality". Take a moment to view our menu for today's 'Daily Fish'.
