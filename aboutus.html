Our Story

The Red Stone Restaurant was built in 1914 on the eastern end of Red Stone Forest by Matthew Smith. A humble log style cabin decorated by red-brown leaves; the Red Stone Restaurant serves as your home away from home. Our family has operated the Red Stone Restaurant since its establishment. We are known far and wide for our 'homely service'.

Our Mission

We aim to serve you! Our family has proudly been feeding local hikers and campers for three generations, and we aim to continue our tradition of family service. Here, everyone is family and we want you to feel right at home. Know that when you come to visit you will be welcomed with all the comforts of home, including our many award-winning smiles.

Chef Ange's Tasty Treats

Chef Ange Smith has served as our family's master chef for 15 years and counting. Over the years she has developed her skills as a master chef and strives to please every hungry customer. She is eager to show you the many delicacies of the Red Stone Forest.

Chef Ange recommends:

  1. Red Fish Medley
  2. Red Stone Burger
  3. The Running Man
  4. Chicken-Seafood Fajita
  5. Cracker Jack Stone Sandwich

The Red Stone Forest

The Red Stone Forest is home to one of the most beautiful camping sites, Camp Flora. Visitors come to bask in the vast wilderness and beautiful scenery. As proud natives to the forest, we are dedicated to the well-being of the forest and strive to have our restaurant act as a voice of the forest. A voice that shows and advocates serenity and harmony with nature.
