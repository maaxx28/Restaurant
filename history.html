The Red Stone

The name 'Red Stone' comes from the oak trees and stones found in the forest. Many of the stones have a shade of autumn red. The trees have an autumn brown color and a texture very similar to the stones. As the restaurant is made from oak found in the forest, it inherited the name, and thus, became the Red Stone Restaurant.
